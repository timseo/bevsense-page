1. Introduction

BevSense LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website www.bevsense.com (the “Website”). This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies, what types of cookies we use, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide analytical data.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of the Website, such as enabling navigation and access to secure areas. They do not require user consent.

b. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Website by collecting data on page visits, time spent on the site, and error messages. We may use services like Google Analytics to analyze this data.

c. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the Website to remember your preferences, such as language settings or login details, to provide a more personalized experience.

d. Advertising and Targeting Cookies

These cookies track your browsing behavior to deliver relevant ads based on your interests. They may be set by third-party advertising partners like Google Ads or Facebook Pixel.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the proper functioning of the Website.
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior to improve our services.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Provide relevant advertisements based on your interests (if applicable).

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our Website may be placed by third-party services, such as analytics tools or advertising platforms. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block or delete cookies.
  • Enable notifications before cookies are stored.
  • Disable third-party cookies.

To modify your settings, visit the help section of your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Internet Explorer
  • Safari
